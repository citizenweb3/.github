## 🌍 Mission

Citizen Web3 is a **non-custodial, self-hosted, off-grid infrastructure provider** and an active builder in the Web3 galaxy.

Born in 2020 (formerly Citizen Cosmos), we operate bare-metal validators across multiple chains, build powerful public-good tools, and now pioneer the integration of **autonomous AI agents** directly into blockchain infrastructure.

We believe blockchains are digital nations — and we build the tools, the infrastructure, and the living intelligence that helps them thrive.

> "Blockchains are digital nations. We build open-source tools, off-grid infrastructure, and autonomous AI agents to support their prosperity."

The most ambitious Web3 explorer on Earth. Real-time validator, mining pool, staking, and network metrics with **live autonomous AI agents** embedded inside the product.

### [Citizen Web3 Staking & Bare-Metal Infrastructure](https://www.citizenweb3.com/staking)
Privacy-first, renewable-energy, off-grid capable validators and full infrastructure stack:
- Endpoints, archives, snapshots, relayers

We don’t just run validators — we **build and integrate autonomous AI teams** for other Web3 projects, DAOs, explorers, wallets, and services.

ValidatorInfo.com is our flagship live example. We help projects design, deploy, and run their own AI workforces for growth, operations, outreach, and innovation.
